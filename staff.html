Flight Operations Department

The Flight Department is responsible for creating and modifying the flight plans and monitoring the routes. It also controls the PIREP validation and deletes any incorrectly submitted flights that do not comply with the Rules & Regs. Likewise, the FD is responsible for creating tours and group flights
Feel free to contact us - fod@wika-airline.de!
